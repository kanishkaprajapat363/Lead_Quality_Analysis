# Lead Quality Analysis using Python & Power BI

## Overview

This project analyzes approximately 3,000 marketing leads to identify lead quality trends, key drivers of lead quality, and optimization opportunities. The goal was to determine whether lead quality could be improved by 20% in exchange for a 20% increase in Cost Per Lead (CPL).

## Objectives

* Analyze lead quality trends over time.
* Identify factors influencing lead quality.
* Evaluate performance across widgets, partners, campaigns, debt levels, and verification scores.
* Recommend strategies to improve lead quality and conversion outcomes.

## Tools & Technologies

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn
* Power BI
* Google Colab

## Key Findings

* Overall Lead Quality: **13.01%**
* Closed Rate: **8.11%**
* Lead quality improved toward the end of the analysis period.
* Widget design significantly impacted lead quality.
* Advertise.com generated the highest-quality leads.
* Call Center leads outperformed online form submissions.
* PhoneScore 5 leads achieved the strongest quality rates.
* Branding showed minimal impact on lead quality.

## Recommendations

* Increase traffic allocation to high-performing widgets.
* Shift budget toward top-performing partners.
* Expand Call Center lead acquisition.
* Prioritize leads with high PhoneScore values.
* Reduce investment in low-performing traffic sources.

## Conclusion

The analysis identified several high-performing segments that exceeded the target lead quality threshold. By reallocating spend and focusing on high-quality acquisition channels, the advertiser can potentially achieve a 20% improvement in lead quality while supporting a higher CPL.
